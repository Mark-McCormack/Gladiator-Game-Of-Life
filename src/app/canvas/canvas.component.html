Gladiator Game of Life

This project uses Conway's "Game of Life" to study how two groups of cells will interact and who will survive. You can set the board to generate two teams and use either the "Step" or "Loop" buttons to watch the game play out.

What is the purpose of this project?

The main goal for this project was to investigate and study how cellular automata interact with one another in groups. Also, I wanted to implement Conway's "Game of Life" in a JavaScript Framework, Angular in this case.

What are the "Upcoming Features"?

For the future, I plan to train the cellular automata to remember patterns which led to the best outcome for different teams, as well as unique strategies for each step in the game. I also plan to implement custom patterns and multiplayer options.
